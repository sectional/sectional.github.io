A structural steel database and section property calculator

Coming SoonFor Windows 7 or later.Other platforms -Beta releases

Sectional is a tool for structural engineers that catalogues properties of structural shapes. It's searchable, sortable, customizable, and lets you create and store your own custom shapes.

Full-featured, right out of the box

Cross-platform

Sectional works across operating systems. You can use it on OS X, Windows, or Linux.

Searchable

Search for the section you're looking for. Sectional filters as you type

Sortable

Sort sections by weight, depth, width, or any other section property.

Export

Export your desired section properties for printing or importing into STAAD.pro

Custom Sections

Design your own custom section. Sectional will calculate the properties for you.

Tags

Tag sections based on the project or structure you're working on.
